King Arthur is a central figure in Great Britian Mythology. King Arthur is a legendary British leader, famous for his role in medieval literature and folklore. He is best known as the king who defended Britain against Saxon invaders and established a utopian realm known as Camelot. Arthur is central to many iconic tales, such as pulling the sword Excalibur from the stone, forming the Knights of the Round Table, and seeking the Holy Grail.
